Waters Under Pressure

A single-device water-literacy team game for teenagers. One phone, tablet, laptop or classroom screen becomes the mission console. Teams read evidence, make decisions and protect the watershed.

▶ Play in English

How it works

  1. Open one shared device.
  2. Split students into 2 or 3 teams.
  3. Pass the device to each team when it is their turn.
  4. Each team answers the same evidence-based challenges.
  5. The game calculates the score automatically.
